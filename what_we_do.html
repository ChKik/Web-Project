What We Do

Where We Focus

At ResQ, our mission is to make a positive impact by focusing on various aspects that shape thriving communities. We accept donations from around the world to support our efforts in assisting people in need, rescuing individuals, and ensuring the well-being of animals. Operating at a national level in Greece, our initiatives span across multiple areas to create a better and more sustainable future.

Economic Wellbeing

We empower communities by providing economic support, fostering entrepreneurship, and creating sustainable income-generating projects. Our goal is to uplift individuals and families, promoting financial stability and resilience.

Health

We prioritize healthcare initiatives that address medical needs, promote preventive care, and enhance overall well-being. From medical camps to health education, we work towards building healthier communities.

Empowerment

Empowering individuals, especially women and marginalized groups, is at the core of our mission. We focus on education, skill development, and creating opportunities that lead to self-reliance and empowerment.

Safety

We are dedicated to ensuring the safety of communities in times of emergencies and disasters. Our initiatives include disaster response, community resilience training, and creating safe spaces for vulnerable populations.

Animal Welfare

Our commitment extends to the well-being of animals. We work towards the rescue, rehabilitation, and protection of animals in need. From street animals to wildlife conservation, we strive to create a compassionate world for all living beings.
